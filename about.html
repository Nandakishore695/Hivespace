About Divya Reddy Solipuram: The Founder of Hive Space

Divya Reddy Solipuram is the visionary founder and driving force behind HIVE, a thriving co-working space located in the heart of Hyderabad. Divya’s journey began with a passion for design, After graduating with a degree in architecture she joined her family business, Quickly rising to the position of Director and overseeing the design of over 3 lakh sq ft of office space. This experience gave her a deep understanding of the needs for businesses. However, Divya saw a gap in the market: a lack of affordable and inspiring workspaces for startups. This realization led to the birth of Hive Space in 2015. Hive is more than just a co-working space;it’s a community. Divya has carefully curated an environment that fosters collaboration, innovation, and growth. Divya’s vision for Hive Space is to provide a comprehensive solution for startups, offering everything from coworking space to essential amenities. Divya’s dedication to empowering entrepreneurs has earned her recognition as the Times of India’s Young Entrepreneur 2022 and also received an honourable award from Blackswan for Women empowerment. The company also received the Times Business Award for Best Coworking Space and Enterprise Office Space in 2023. Divya’s journey shows us that limitations are often self-imposed. Diagnosed with congenital Kidney disease at a young age, she has faced health challenges throughout her life. Yet, she never let these challenges define her. Instead, she used them as fuel, inspiring countless others with her unwavering spirit.
